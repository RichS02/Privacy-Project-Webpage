Privacy Project User Study

Stony Brook University

Purpose

The purpose of this user study is to evaluate how well our system can determine your view towards various entities (people, places, organizations, etc.) based on the articles you read. Our system will periodically extract articles from your history and process them locally on your computer. We do not receive any of this information.

You will then submit a survey each week of how well the system identifies your view towards certain entities. No personal information will be sent in the survey, only the scores of how well the system performs will be sent.

The ultimate goal is to develop a local application that can inform a user of what can be learned from their personal information. The application could then recommend steps to preserve neutrality/anonymity.

How to Participate

To participate, you must be at least 18 years of age. You will also need to use Google Chrome on your Mac or Windows PC and have your chrome history available. If your history is already cleared, that is okay, just make sure not to clear it during the study period. We will not receive any of your history data, more details explained in the next section.

  1. Send us an email letting us know you would like to participate here.
  2. Download the application from the Downloads Section at the bottom of the page and agree to the consent form.
  3. Run the application. You must leave it running for the duration of the study. If you do close it, simply run the application again and the system will resume where it left off.
  4. Send us a survey each week by opening the "Dashboard" and select "Start Survey". The survey will ask you to answer if our system correctly predicted your view towards various entities.

What Data will be Accessed?

When the application first launches it will create a folder in your home directory called "com.stony-brook.nlp.privacy-project". It will copy your chrome history to this folder and extract the articles from your history links periodically (the application should consume little CPU as it is designed to sleep often). The application will store all data in this folder and no where else. All data will be processed locally on your computer and no data will be sent to us.

When you are ready to send us a survey for the week, you will press the "Open Dashboard" button which will open a web interface locally in your browser. From there you will press the "Start Survey" button and follow the instructions. We will receive the survey using a standard "Google Survey" form. No personal information will be sent, only the scores of how well the system performed. Furthermore, you will be able to see the data we will be receiving when you open the Google Survey.

How to Uninstall

If at any moment you want to stop the study or you finished the study, then simply close the application and delete it. No additional processes run in the background. Lastly, delete the "com.stony-brook.nlp.privacy-project" in your home directory.
