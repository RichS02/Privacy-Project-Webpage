Privacy Project User Study

Stony Brook University

Purpose

The purpose of this user study is to evaluate how well our system can determine your view towards various entities (people, places, organizations, etc.) based on the articles you read. Our system will periodically download articles from your history and process them locally on your computer. We do not receive any of this information.

You will then submit a survey each week of how well the system identifies your view towards certain entities. No personal information will be sent in the survey, only the scores of how well the system performs will be sent.

The ultimate goal is to develop a local application that can inform a user of what can be learned from their personal information. The application could then recommend steps to preserve neutrality/anonymity.

Our system is completely Open Source.

How to Participate

To participate, you must be at least 18 years of age and read online articles (news, blogs, etc.) for at least 30 minutes each day on average. You will also need to use the Google Chrome Browser on your Mac (Sierra OS or higher) or Windows PC (Windows 10) and have your chrome history available. If your history is already cleared, that is okay, just make sure not to clear it during the study period. You must also have an internet connection for the system to download the articles from your history. We will not receive any of your history data, more details explained in the next section.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Send us an email letting us know you would like to participate to mbastan@cs.stonybrook.edu

  2. Download the application from the Downloads Section at the bottom of the page and read/agree to the consent form.

  3. Unzip and then run the application.

    4. To run the application for Mac, just run the "privacy-project.app." For Windows, run the "privacy-project.exe" which can be found inside the downloaded folder (Note: Don't move anything inside this folder).

    If you get a security warning, this is normal. You can right click and open the application to bypass this.

  4. Send us a survey each week by opening the "Dashboard" and select "Start Survey." The survey will ask you to answer if our system correctly predicted your view towards various entities.

    You must leave the application running for the duration of the study. If you do quit it, simply run the application again and the system will resume where it left off.

What Data will be Accessed?

When the application first launches it will create a folder in your home directory called "com.stony-brook.nlp.privacy-project". It will copy your chrome history to this folder and download the articles from your history links periodically (the application should consume little CPU as it is designed to sleep often). The application will store all data in this folder and no where else. The application will require internet access to download the articles from your history. All data will be processed locally on your computer and no data will be sent to us.

When you are ready to send us a survey for the week, you will press the "Open Dashboard" button which will open a web interface locally in your browser. From there you will press the "Start Survey" button and follow the instructions. We will receive the survey using a standard "Google Survey" form. No personal information will be sent, only the scores of how well the system performed. Furthermore, you will be able to see the data we will be receiving when you open the Google Survey.

How to Uninstall

If at any moment you want to stop the study or you finished the study, then simply press the "uninstall button" in the dashboard and then delete the original application that you downloaded at the start of the study.

If you would like to verify everything was uninstalled, the following files should be deleted.

macOS

  1. /Users/USER/com.stony-brook.nlp.privacy-project
  2. /Users/USER/Library/LaunchAgents/com.stony-brook.nlp.privacy-project-agent.plist
  3. The privacy-project.app that you downloaded at the start of the study

Windows

  1. C:\Users\USER\com.stony-brook.nlp.privacy-project
  2. C:\Users\USER\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\privacy-project
  3. The privacy-project folder that you downloaded at the start of the study
